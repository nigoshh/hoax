HOAX booking

Welcome to HOAX booking! Please note, all date/times (also those that you input) are in UTC.

You can create an account to become part of a community. After you log in, you can share a resource you have access to with one or many communities. You can book a resource that your community has access to, and you can create an invoice to pay for your bookings.

At this GitHub repo you can find more detailed documentation (and the app's source code).
